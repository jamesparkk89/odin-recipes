Tequila Sunrise

The Tequila Sunrise cocktail, with its bright striations of color, evokes a summer sunrise. This classic drink has only three ingredients—tequila, grenadine and orange juice—and is served unmixed to preserve the color of each layer.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces blacno tequila
  • 4 ounces orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/4th ounce grenadine
  • Garnish: orange slice
  • Garnish: cherry

  1. Step 1

    Add the tequila and then the orange juice to a chilled highball glass filled with ice.

  2. Step 2

    Top with the grenadine, which will sink to the bottom of the glass, creating a layered effect.

  3. Step 3

    Garnish with an orange slice and a cherry.
